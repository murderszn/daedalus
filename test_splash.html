Our Story

In a summer of inspiration, Josh Johnson and Sierhah Price envisioned a company to revolutionize education. Ten days later, Daedalus Research was born. Named after the ingenious creator of Greek mythology, Daedalus symbolizes innovation, adaptability, and bold problem-solving. Starting as an ed-tech firm, our mission was clear: empower schools and teachers to transform education through cutting-edge technology and visionary solutions. Today, we bring that same passion to data analytics, reporting, applications, and beyond, striving to change the world one solution at a time.
