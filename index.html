Our Story

In a summer of inspiration, Joshua Johnson and Sierhah Price envisioned a bold idea: to harness the power of multi-agent AI and revolutionize workflows across industries. Driven by a shared passion for innovation and a desire to reduce operational inefficiencies, they set out to create a company that would empower organizations to focus on their core missions. Just ten days later, Daedalus Solutions was born.

Inspired by Daedalus, the legendary innovator of Greek mythology, our name embodies ingenuity, adaptability, and the courage to tackle complex challenges. Our mission is clear: to combine human expertise with the power of artificial intelligence, building solutions that reduce costs, streamline processes, and unlock new opportunities for growth. From automating repetitive tasks to pioneering AI-driven strategies, Daedalus Solutions is here to help organizations thrive.
